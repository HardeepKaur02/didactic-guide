OUR MENU

Gulab Jamun

"Queen of desserts" as they say it. Soft,small n juicy balls, deep fried and dunked into a sugar syrup.

Barfi

Essentially a solidified form of a milk based pudding. A sweet batter,thickened and cut into pieces.

Rasgulla

Soft,small and juicy ball shaped dumplings,made of chhena n semolina dough, dunked into a sugary syrup.
